About Pearl Commercial Tower

Pearl Commercial Tower is a premium commercial development offering world-class office spaces designed for contemporary businesses. Located in the heart of Gandhipuram, this 8-storey structure provides 32 meticulously planned office units across approximately 1,20,000 sq ft of built-up area.

Each office space is equipped with modern amenities, high-speed connectivity, and sustainable features. The tower features dedicated parking, 24/7 security, backup power systems, and modern fire safety infrastructure. The architectural design maximizes natural light and ventilation while maintaining a professional ambiance.

Pearl Commercial Tower represents the perfect investment for businesses looking for premium workspace in a central location with excellent connectivity and infrastructure support.
