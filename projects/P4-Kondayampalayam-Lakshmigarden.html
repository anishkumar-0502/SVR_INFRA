About Kondayampalayam - Lakshmi Garden

Kondayampalayam Lakshmi Garden is a premium residential property located in the serene Lakshmi Garden area near Vishwa Brahma Nagar. This well-designed 2BHK villa offers modern living with spacious interiors and excellent amenities for comfortable family living.

The property features a well-planned layout with multiple bedrooms, attached bathrooms, a modern kitchen, and a spacious portico. The villa is built on a generous plot with a water tank capacity of 6000 liters and a borewell for sustainable water management.

Located at the West site North door entrance, this property provides excellent accessibility and is situated in a quiet, established residential locality with all modern conveniences within easy reach.
