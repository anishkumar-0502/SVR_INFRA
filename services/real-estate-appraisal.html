Professional Real Estate Appraisal

SVR provides comprehensive and accurate property appraisals using industry-standard methodologies. Our certified appraisers deliver reliable valuations for residential, commercial, and industrial properties across the region.

Our Appraisal Methodology

01
Property Inspection

Thorough physical inspection and documentation of property condition, improvements, and unique characteristics.

02
Market Research

Comprehensive analysis of comparable sales, market trends, and economic factors affecting property value.

03
Valuation Analysis

Application of three appraisal approaches (sales comparison, cost, income) to develop well-supported property value.

04
Report Delivery

Detailed appraisal report compliant with USPAP standards delivered with professional recommendations and analysis.
