About SVR

SVR Infrastructures & Builders is a leading construction and infrastructure firm based in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu. Founded by visionary entrepreneur Mr. Ajith Kumar A, we have been delivering premium infrastructure and construction solutions with unwavering commitment to quality and innovation since 2014.

Infrastructure Excellence Since 2014

Building Quality Infrastructure, Creating Lasting Value

SVR Infrastructures & Builders brings together a team of 11-50 dedicated professionals with expertise in architecture, interior design, landscape design, commercial real estate, and infrastructure development. Our commitment to quality, sustainability, and innovation has made us a trusted partner for premium construction projects across Coimbatore and beyond.

With over a decade of experience, we have successfully completed 100+ projects ranging from independent houses and apartments to commercial buildings and large-scale infrastructure developments. Our passion for excellence drives us to consistently exceed client expectations and deliver projects that stand the test of time.
